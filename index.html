01. STRATEGIC MISSION

The V-2 UN DÉSIGNÉ initiative represents the pinnacle of modern aerospace education. We are dedicated to providing an exhaustive framework for the study of advanced missile propulsion and autonomous drone navigation. Our program is built on the philosophy that the future of defense and exploration lies in the mastery of sub-orbital ballistics and real-time electronic flight control. Under the guidance of Mohammed Rayen Bouazizi, this repository serves as a permanent archive for engineering excellence.

02. EXTENDED CURRICULUM

Ballistic Trajectory Modeling

In-depth analysis of launch phase aerodynamics, including the transition from laminar to turbulent flow. Students study the impact of Earth's rotation (Coriolis effect) on long-range missile accuracy and the optimization of multi-stage separation timing.

Propulsion Thermodynamics

Comprehensive coverage of chemical rocket engines, focusing on the Rankine cycle and combustion stability. The program explores the molecular properties of liquid vs. solid propellants and the thermal limits of nozzle materials.
